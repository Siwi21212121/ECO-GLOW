Our Mission

Millions of tons of organic waste are discarded yearly, contributing to pollution and methane emissions. Eco Glow transforms this underutilized resource into compost and innovative skincare products.

Reduce Waste

We aim to divert organic waste from landfills by providing practical, easy-to-implement solutions for every household.

Enrich Gardens

Turn kitchen scraps into nutrient-rich compost that will help your plants thrive and create a sustainable ecosystem in your garden.

Natural Skincare

Discover how common organic waste items contain valuable compounds that can become part of your natural skincare routine.

How to Use Eco Glow

Step 1: Prepare Your Waste

Take a clear photo of your organic waste or note its name (e.g., banana peels).

Step 2: Enter or Upload

Type the waste name or drag your photo into the drop zone in the Scan tab.

Step 3: View Results

See the waste type and suggestions for composting and skincare uses.

Step 4: Take Action

Use the suggestions to repurpose your waste or contact us to get involved!
